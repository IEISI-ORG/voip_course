## Strategies IT security managers actually use

From a qualitative multiple-case study of VoIP security managers (Bernard, Walden Univ., 2022) —
four recurring themes:

1. **Best practices for VoIP security** — encryption-first (signalling + media), hardening, patching.
2. **Choose a secure VoIP provider** — due-diligence on the upstream/trunk, not just your own edge.
3. **Follow VoIP security recommendations** — standards/vendor guidance, kept current.
4. **Awareness of future concerns** — the threat set keeps evolving; revisit continuously.

Practitioner recommendations: **early-breach monitoring**, **staff & customer training**, and
**masking card numbers spoken over the phone** (→ this course's BF8 DTMF/PAN masking).
